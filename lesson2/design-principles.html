Contrast

Ibanez Guitars

ibanez.com

The Ibanez website looks great. Here is an excellent example demonstrating the design principle of contrast. The white font really pops on top of the attractive, darker background.

Fitt's Law

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints
churchofjesuschrist.org

Fitt's law predicts that the time required to move to a target area is a function of the ratio between the distance to the target and the width of the target. The design of The Church Of Jesus Christ Of Latter Days Saints website takes this into account. On the website there are several articles you can navigate to, scrolling from the top down. Each large square image is actually a clickable button which links the to the articles. This makes the website very easy to use, and it works well on a mobile device.

Alignment

Gibson Brands

gibson.com

In addition to using the design concepts of negative space and proximity, the Gibson website uses both vertical and horizontal alignment. The result is a well organized, attractive layout that provides a great user experience.
