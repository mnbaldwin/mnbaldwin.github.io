Is It Spring Time Yet?

by Lamont Sanford

Well, is it? For the residence of Soda Springs that may sound like a trick question. Technically, yes the spring equinox, also known and the vernal equinox, actually occurred on March 19th this year. This marks the time when the sun shines directly on the equator, giving a roughly equal amount of daylight and darkness at night, depending on where you live the planet you are. In fact, this was the earliest vernal equinox in 124 years.

That all sounds pretty good right? Well, for residence in our area it will continue to feel like winter for at least a few more weeks. We will have wet snow, flurries, mixed with a few sunny days, and temperatures will remain in the 30's and possibly low 40's, at least into the first weeks of April.

So for now, spring may have to remain a hopeful state of mind. Don't worry, we'll be breaking out the shorts and sun screen soon enough.
