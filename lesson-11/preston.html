Go Fly a Kite With Napolean Dynamite

by Fred G. Sanford

Are you getting spring fever? Is it more like cabin fever? Are you going insane, self isolating, and eating canned green beans? Do you need a reason to shave? Are you tough enough to wear shorts in 45 degree weather? Well, we have just the answer for you. Go fly a kite!

When? Every weekend in March and April. Where? Right accross the street from Napolean Dynamite's house. You know, the famous field where the heroic cow was hunted down by the neighbor, right in front of all the students on the school bus.

Will Napolean Dynamite be there? Absolutely not. Are you crazy? Don't worry, Kip and Uncle Rico look alikes will be there mingling and selling forged autographs.

There will be loads of food and fun. On the menu:

  • Buffet of Quesadillas (case-uh-dill-uhs)
  • Mountain of Egg Salad Sandwiches
  • Panfried Steaks (prime Holstein beef)

To quench your thirst choose from these delicious, luke warm options:

  • Unpasturized, onion flavored milk
  • Orange juice, fortfied with locally raised, raw chicken eggs
  • Free refills!

Come on down. We can't wait to see you there!
