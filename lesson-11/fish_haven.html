The Bear Lake Monster

by James Douglas Morrison

Say what you want, but according to some Fish Haven residents the Bear Lake Monster is alive and thriving. The most recent sighting was reported on March 21st, 2020, by Canard Yarner, a 30 year old Fish Haven resident and business owner.

Mr. Yarner stated he was on the lake in a small boat 12:30 pm, when he saw the creature. He stated the creature followed him for several minutes, repeatedly surfacing, swimming around and underneath the boat. He described the creature's appearance as large, pail yellow-green, serpert like, roughly 30 to 40 feat long, with arms, legs, and head a like an aligator.

This seems to align with reports of the Bear Lake Monster going back centuries, originating with natives, and later being confirmed by settlers in the Bear Lake area. Does the Bear Lake Monster really exist? Experts say as the waters of Bear Lake warm up, we can expect the creature to become more active, and may increase the liklihood of further sightings. You may be able to find out for yourself.
