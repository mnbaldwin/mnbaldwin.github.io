Target Audience, Personas, and Scenarios

Target Audience

The target audience of the truWeather.com web site is described below:

  • Who: This website is for people of all ages, education levels, income levels and backgrounds. It is targeted towards anyone who is interested in seeking weather related information.
  • Age: 8 to 100
  • Motivation: Reading weathers forecasts, getting accurate realtime weather updates, and consuming weather related content.
